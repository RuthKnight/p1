Eligibility

We are on the lookout for someone who has understood and comprehended the responsibilities, duties, expectations and skills that are a part of the internship-job description.


Interns in general are expected to understand the company better by working closely with members from different teams and assist the company staff by undertaking duties such as research and data capturing as instructed by the different teams.


Responsibilities

  • Work closely with different departments to complete all the tasks assigned.
  • Report to supervisors upon the completion of the task and seek clarification.
  • Perform any research work as instructed by the supervisor.
  • Post regularly on social media as instructed and prepare the copy for the same.
  • Help with producing images for posting on social media.

Requirements

  • Final year undergraduate students or recent graduates.
  • Willingness to work with different departments in the company.
  • Appreciable communication skills-verbal and written.
  • Proficient in Microsoft Office.
  • Ability to handle multiple tasks in one go.
  • Must be someone who thrives under pressure.

Intern with us

This internship has been carefully planned out so as to enable the interns with maximal skills in a limited period of time whilst also focusing on their Social Emotional Learning (SEL).
